Description

These tracks annotate repetitive regions identified by the following tools:

  • Tandem Repeats Finder (TRF), which locates simple tandem repeats (possibly imperfect repeats)
  • RepeatMasker program, which screens DNA sequences for interspersed repeats and low complexity DNA sequences
  • WindowMasker

    • The original gasAcu1 tracks were obtained from the UCSC Genome Browser and then lifted to gasAcu1-4.
