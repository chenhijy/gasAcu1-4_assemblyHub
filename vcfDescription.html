Description

This track displays genomic variants predicted by DNA sequencing data of 227 natural stickleback populations from the Pacific and Atlantic ocean basins. Please see Roberts Kingman et al. 2021) for complete information about Materials and Methods. A copy of the Variant Call File (VCF) underlying this track is available through FigShare.
